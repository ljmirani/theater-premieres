Theater Premieres

A Guide to the new plays of my favorite theatres

By Jaya Mirani

One of my favorite things to do is to go to the theater. Sitting in the audience, not knowing what to expect, the immediacy of it, witnessing what happens on stage in real time - I really love it.

The summer break of the theater scene in Germany has just started. So I wanted to take a look at the new plays for the next season, which starts in September. I will be abroad for most of next year, so in this project I want to check which premieres I can see while I am home. I also want to see what themes the new plays will cover.

For this project I chose three of my favorite (and easy to analyse) theatres:

Gorki Theater in Berlin.

The Schaubühne in Berlin.

The Kammerspiele in Munich.

I will not be in Munich and Berlin most of the time during the next few months, so I wanted to check which plays I could see while I am there. I will be there in September, December and April.
